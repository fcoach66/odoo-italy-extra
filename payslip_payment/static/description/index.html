This module allows you to register payment in the pay slip, by default odoo does not provide this functionality, we register the payment and reconcile the accounting entries automatically


Payable Accounting Entry

By default odoo is posting accounting entry if you are configuring the credit and debit accounts in salary rules


Confirm Batch Payslips

you can confirm the batch payslips by clicking on confirm payslips button


Register Payment in Payslip

Register payment by clicking on register payment button in the payslip record


Register Payment in Batch

you can register the payment in batchwise by clicking on register payment button in payslip batch


Payment Log

Payment entry is logged in each payslip
